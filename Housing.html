Special Intrest/ Etc. Housing

    Frederick Douglass Leadership House:

    A cultural house geared toward University of Rochester's Black and Latino Students. Focused on building leaders and empowring students of color, this house is a great option for those looking to get involved with the Greater Rochester community and learn to follow in the steps of great colored leaders!

    Sigma Alpha Mu

    Develop strong fraternal values with the brothers of Sigma Alpha Mu, who focus on service and leadership, with a deep appreciation for Jewish Heritage.
