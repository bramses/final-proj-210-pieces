Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I finished the registration, but I get an error message. What do I do?
A: Either your information (First name, last name, ID number) is incorrect or the housing option you chose was unavailable. Check your information and refresh the page and try again.

Q: What happens if the housing option I want runs out before I get to register?
A: Before registration opens, make a plan with several backup options in the case that your preferred option is filled. If it is, check all the other housing options to see which ones are available.
